Vue.js was created by Evan You in 2014. It focuses on the view layer of your application and can be easily integrated into existing projects. Vue uses an HTML-based template syntax and reactive data binding to render dynamic interfaces efficiently.

üöÄ Evolution of Vue.js

  • 2014: Vue.js was released as a lightweight alternative to Angular and React.
  • 2016: Vue 2 introduced better performance and a robust ecosystem.
  • 2020: Vue 3 brought the Composition API, improved TypeScript support, and faster rendering.
  • Today: Vue continues to grow with strong community support and modern tooling.

‚ú® Why is Vue.js Important?

  • Easy to learn for beginners yet powerful enough for large apps.
  • Two-way data binding with a simple and declarative syntax.
  • Flexible architecture: use it for small widgets or full apps.
  • Excellent documentation and developer experience.
  • Supported by a rich ecosystem (Vue Router, Vuex, Pinia, Vite).
